Web Development

Website development is the process of building and maintaining websites. It involves a combination of front-end design (what users see) and back-end programming (how the site works behind the scenes). A well-developed website ensures a smooth user experience, fast performance, mobile responsiveness, and high security. Web development also includes integrating databases, implementing SEO strategies, and deploying the site on reliable servers. Whether you're creating a personal blog or a full-scale e-commerce platform, professional web development is key to online success.
